Interactive Augmented Sculpture

During this ‘live’ project, the 3rd Year of Interaction Design were tasked with staging a show at the Glasgow Vintage Vehicle Trust. We presented a series of works including interactives, projections and Virtual Reality experiences.

For this event, I collaborated with class mate Hana Seo to design, develop and install an Augmented Sculpture. The main inspiration came from the bus seat patterns we see everyday but do not acknowledge for their elusive and blending nature. A database of patterns was sourced from Have a Seat!.
