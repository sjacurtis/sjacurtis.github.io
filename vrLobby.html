PovertyAlliance///VR Lobby

In a collaboration with Glasgow’s Poverty Alliance, I developed a VR prototype for a lobby environment. The aim of this project was to convert the Poverty Alliance’s web presence into an immersive medium. VR has allowed the presentation of museum events (and similar conventions) to anyone equipped with the appropriate hardware, regardless of location. Companies and brands can now engage a wider audience by holding these “virtual events”

Users are greeted with a large-scale web browser providing a live stream of the Poverty Alliance’s activities. The main lobby area includes:

  • Wall panel infographics
  • Audio commentary widget
  • Interactive data visualisation prototype: Unemployment in Glasgow map

Software used:

  • Unreal Engine 4
  • 3ds Max
  • Adobe Photoshop
