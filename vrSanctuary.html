VR Sanctuary//3D animation and narrative concept

I channeled my love for sci-fi and visual effects into this ‘Virtual Sculpture’ project, building from early sketches/concept to a fully realised 3D animation. This project was created with Virtual Reality potential in mind, and will be an ongoing visual theme as I explore this new technology.

I created a website: VR Sanctuary to present the concept and background story behind the artwork, including an interactive 360 panorama and sketchbook development gallery.

Software used:

  • 3ds Max (Modeling and Animation)
  • Adobe Photoshop (Textures/Concept Art)
  • Abobe After Effects (Concept video)
  • Adobe Premiere Pro (Concept video)
